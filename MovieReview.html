Breaking Bad — My Simple Review

Breaking Bad tells the story of Walter White, a high-school chemistry teacher who changes into the feared Heisenberg. The show mixes crime, drama, and strong acting. It is tense, smart, and hard to stop watching.

The writing stays tight across the seasons. Small choices become big consequences — that makes the show feel real and powerful. The characters feel lived-in and the dialogues are sharp.

Why I Like It

  • Great acting — Bryan Cranston & Aaron Paul are excellent.
  • Strong character change and tension.
  • Good mix of dark moments and small human scenes.

My Rating:(5/5)

Must watch if you love great TV stories.
